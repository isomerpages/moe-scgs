# **Parent Volunteer**

Parents of prospective students are welcome to apply and join SCGS as Parent Volunteers to learn more about the school while volunteering your time to support the school. Upon completion of at least 40 hours of voluntary service to the school by 30 June of the year of your childâ€™s P1 registration, you would be eligible to register your child under Phase 2B. Please visit MOE website for more information of P1 registration process and Phase 2B eligibility.

**Parent Volunteers Application**

The Parent Volunteer application window is currently closed.  If your child is born between 2 Jan 2019 and 1 Jan 2020 (both dates inclusive), you may apply for Parent Volunteer online from mid-Jun 2023.  You may refer to the following timeline to understand the procedural flow for Parent Volunteers.
