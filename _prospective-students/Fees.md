# **Fees**

MOE will not be making any changes to school fees for PR and IS (ASEAN) students in 2021. There will be moderate adjustments to the school fees for IS (Non-ASEAN) students in 2021. In SCGS, we strongly believe that no deserving student should be deprived of an education. We have in place a comprehensive school-based Financial Assistance scheme to support our students whose monthly gross household income is less than $12,000 or monthly per capita income is less than $3,000. Application forms for financial assistance are available from&nbsp;[SCGS website](/prospective-students/financial-assistance/)&nbsp;and General Office.

School fees and miscellaneous fees are due and payable quarterly in advance and deducted through GIRO.

Fees, once paid or deducted, will not be refunded.
